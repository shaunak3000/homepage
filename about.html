About

I hold a PhD in Industrial Engineering from Purdue University (2013), and have spent the years since building a career that deliberately refuses to choose between theory and practice.

My research has been published in FT50-ranked journals including the Journal of Operations Management, Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control, Journal of Operational Research Society, and Annals of Operations Research.

My industry work has spanned credit underwriting at scale, fintech analytics, and operations strategy for Fortune 500 companies across Europe and the United States — from modeling CCAR stress tests at Discover Financial to leading credit risk strategy at Jenius Bank to pricing and risk at Shopify.

I managed a team of over 10 researchers at TU Eindhoven and was an award-winning teacher, responsible for the Business Economics elective package including Microeconomics, International Risk Management, Accounting, and Financial Management.
